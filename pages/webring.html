VOCALOID WEBRING
Welcome to the Vocaloid Webring! This webring is for any and all Vocaloid enjoyers!

RULES
The rules are quite simple!
  • You must be a vocaloid fan
  • No bigotry or discrimination of any kind (lgbtphobia, racism, sexism, etc.)
  • No nsfw sites allowed
  • Your site doesn't have to be hosted on neocities
  • Custom widget styling is allowed
  • You must stan miku
JOIN
Interested in joining? Shoot me an email at a@adilene.net and use the following format:
